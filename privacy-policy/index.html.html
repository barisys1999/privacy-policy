Cosmo Hop! - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/12/2025

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how BarisByteGames ("we", "our") collects, uses, and shares user data for the mobile game Cosmo Hop!.

2. Information We Collect

A. In-App Data: Game progress, scores, settings, and gameplay preferences.

B. Technical Data: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), IP address, and mobile network information.

C. Analytics Data: Game usage statistics (session duration, most played levels, etc.) — stored in an anonymized or aggregated form.

D. Payment & Financial Data: If in-app purchases are made, payment processing is handled by third-party providers; we do not store payment details.

3. How We Collect Data

Data is collected via in-app forms, automatic telemetry, and third-party SDKs (e.g., analytics, advertising). Third-party services we use include:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics for Firebase (or other services you use).
  • Advertising: AdMob / Unity Ads (if applicable).
  • Payments: Google Play / App Store (for in-app purchases).

4. How We Use Data

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the game.
  • To improve user experience (analysis and A/B testing).
  • To display ads and measure ad performance (if applicable).
  • To provide customer support and respond to user inquiries.

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data may be shared in the following cases:

  • With service providers (analytics, advertising, payments).
  • As required by law or for security purposes with authorized authorities.

Data shared with third parties is generally anonymized or presented as aggregated statistics.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app and related web pages may use cookies or similar tracking technologies for advertising and analytics purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

If the game is directed to children under the age of 13, parental consent may be required for certain features. If the target audience includes children, please let us know (or we will adjust this section) — we will ensure compliance with applicable laws.

8. Security

We use industry-standard technical and administrative measures to protect user data. However, no internet-connected system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. User Rights

Users have rights to access, correct, delete, and restrict the processing of their data. To make such requests, please contact us at barisys17@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or here. The latest update date is shown at the top of this page.
