BarisByteGames - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/12/2025

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how BarisByteGames (\"we\", \"our\") collects, uses, and shares user data across the mobile games and apps we publish (collectively, the \"Games\"). This policy applies to all Games published by BarisByteGames unless a specific Game lists its own separate privacy policy. If a Game has its own policy, that policy will apply to that Game in addition to this corporate policy.

2. Scope

This policy covers data collection and usage for all games published by BarisByteGames, including but not limited to: game progress, gameplay telemetry, analytics, advertising, and in-app purchases. We may update the list of Games from time to time; for a current list of published titles visit our website or contact us.

3. Information We Collect

A. In-App Data: Game progress, scores, settings, and gameplay preferences.

B. Technical Data: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), IP address, and mobile network information.

C. Analytics Data: Game usage statistics (session duration, most played levels, crash reports) — stored in an anonymized or aggregated form where possible.

D. Advertising & Advertising Identifiers: When ads are served, advertising identifiers may be used to deliver relevant ads and measure performance.

E. Payment Data: For in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by third-party providers (Google Play / App Store). We do not store users' full payment details.

4. How We Collect Data

Data is collected via the Games themselves, in-app forms, automatic telemetry, and third-party SDKs (e.g., analytics, advertising). Third-party services we commonly use include, but are not limited to: Google Analytics for Firebase, AdMob, Unity Ads, and platform payment processors. Specific Games may use additional third-party services — such services will be listed in the Game’s in-app or store listing where required.

5. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Games.
  • Analyze usage, perform A/B tests, and improve gameplay and user experience.
  • Deliver and measure ads (where applicable).
  • Process purchases and provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against abuse or fraud.

6. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (analytics, advertising, hosting, payment processors).
  • Platform providers (Google Play, Apple App Store) as required for purchases and distribution.
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Where practical, shared data is anonymized or aggregated.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Games and related web pages may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Games are not intentionally directed at children under the age of 13. If a Game is directed at children, or if we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under the applicable minimum age without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such data and comply with applicable laws.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and administrative measures to protect personal data. However, no internet-connected system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Transfers

We may transfer and process data outside the user's country of residence. By using our Games you agree to such transfers where permitted by law.

11. User Rights

Users may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of their data depending on local law. To make a request, please contact us at barisys17@gmail.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will post the updated Effective Date above and, where appropriate, notify users through the Games or other means.
